Update your service worker to enable E2EE message decryption.

Step 1: Unregister Old Service Worker

Remove the old service worker that has the bug.

Step 2: Clear Cache & Reload

After unregistering, you need to hard refresh to clear the cache.

Or press: Ctrl+Shift+R (Windows/Linux) or Cmd+Shift+R (Mac)

Step 3: Re-register for Notifications

After reloading, visit the page where you originally registered (admin-setup.html or guest-register.html) and register again. This will install the new service worker.
